How RetinaVista Works

Image Capture

High-resolution retinal images are captured using state-of-the-art cameras.

Image Processing

Advanced algorithms enhance the images to highlight key features relevant to diagnosing retinopathy.

AI Detection

Our custom-built machine learning models analyze the processed images to identify signs of diabetic retinopathy.

Results Delivery

Results are compiled into a detailed report, which includes both the diagnostic outcome and recommended next steps.
